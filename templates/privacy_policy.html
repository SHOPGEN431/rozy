FileYourLLC.online Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how FileYourLLC.online ("we," "our," or "us") handles information when you visit our website. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Introduction

FileYourLLC.online is a comprehensive directory that provides information about LLC formation services and local business providers. Our mission is to help entrepreneurs and business owners find the resources they need to establish and grow their businesses.

We understand that privacy is a fundamental right, and we are committed to protecting yours. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and protection of information when you use our website.

By using FileYourLLC.online, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our website.

We Do Not Collect Personal Information

No Data Collection

FileYourLLC.online does not collect, store, or process any personal information from visitors to our website.

No Tracking

We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any other technology to monitor your browsing activity.

Our Commitment to Privacy

At FileYourLLC.online, we believe that your privacy should never be compromised for the sake of convenience or profit. We have designed our website to function without collecting any personal information from our visitors.

This means you can browse our directory, read reviews, compare services, and access all the information we provide without worrying about your personal data being collected, stored, or shared.

Information We Do NOT Collect

To be completely transparent about our privacy practices, here is a comprehensive list of the types of information we do not collect from our website visitors:

Personal Information

Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personal identifiers.

Browsing Data

IP addresses, browser information, or website usage patterns.

Cookies

We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies.

Location Data

Geographic location or location-based information.

Financial Information

Credit card details, bank information, or payment data.

Third-Party Data

Information from third-party sources or social media platforms.

How Our Directory Operates

Public Information Only

Our directory contains only publicly available information about LLC formation services and local business providers.

Direct Links

We provide direct links to service providers' websites where you can access their services and privacy policies.

Our Data Sources

The information displayed on FileYourLLC.online comes from publicly available sources, including:

  • Official websites of LLC formation services
  • Public business directories and databases
  • State government websites and resources
  • Public reviews and ratings from various platforms

We regularly update our information to ensure accuracy, but we recommend that you verify all details directly with the service providers before making any decisions.

Third-Party Services and Links

Our website contains links to external websites and services that are not operated by us. These third-party services may have their own privacy policies and data collection practices.

When you click on a link to an external website, you will be directed to that site. We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of every site you visit.

We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

Children's Privacy

FileYourLLC.online does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Our website is designed for business owners and entrepreneurs who are typically 18 years of age or older.

If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from children without verification of parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information from our servers.
